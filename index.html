Safety


In many states, a bow and arrow are considered
a firearm, and the same rules and regulations that
apply to firearms also apply to bows and arrows.


When shooting during a class, event, or individual
session, it is important to listen for the OK to begin
shooting. Everyone must shoot at the same time and
remove their arrows at the same time.


Archers are required to wear closed-toe footwear and
clothing that is not too loose.
